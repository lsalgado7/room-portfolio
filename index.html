Project Larry

Tech Skills: Python, Machine Learning, Git/GitHub
Soft Skills: Organization during crunch time, adaptability

For QuackHacks 2024 (the University of Oregon's annual hackathon), my small tearm worked to create a interactive & educational game about data collection. We placed 5th out of 25 teams, with 100+ participants from 3 colleges! We utilized ML to extract human movement through webcam, and made a small interactive game (like a very lightweight Just Dance vibe) and at the end of playing the game, it would tell you that we had collected your movement data! The purpose was to educate people on how even with games, people can extract a lot of data from using their technology.
